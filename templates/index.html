Termites as Urban Pest


Termites are important urban pests in Thailand, they damage houses, wood structures, papers, trees, crops. Termites are social insects, a mature colony contains millions of individuals and excavate tunnels with >100m in distance and infest multiple houses, which cause serious economic loss. However, due to their cryptic life-style, it is not easy to manage household termite problems and the cost of management is highest among house pests.

Reproduction of Termites


Worker and soldier are castes commonly observed in wood, while the winged termites appear only in dispersal flight season. Once winged termites enter houses, they may establish colonies and become new kings and queens of the colony.

About Termite Identification Service


Termite Identification Service is founded by Dr. Chun-I Chiu, Department of Entomology and Plant Pathology of Chiang Mai University. Termite Identification Service aims to help Thai residents to identify termite pest species and analyze termite composition and distribution in Thailand.
